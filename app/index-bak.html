About Me

Hello! My name is Andrew Oh and this is my photography portfolio website.

I grew up in the San Francisco Bay Area and attended UC San Diego where I double majored in Music with an emphasis in Jazz Studies & Cognitive Science with a specialization in Human Computer Interaction.

At UCSD, I worked as a photographer and eventually the photo editor of the UCSD Guardian as well as a photographer for the California Institute for Telecommunications and Information Technology (Calit2).

I currently reside in sunny San Diego. While I mainly work as a web developer for UCSD's Campus Web Office, I also do some work as a freelance photographer for anyone who needs my services. Please look below for any inquiries.
